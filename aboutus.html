Our Mission

Our mission is to catch the Stray dogs of the city which is choosen under a mission. We vaccinate and sterilize them and leave those dogs on the same safe place where they can live peacefully.

Our Values

We are recognized with Animal Welfare Board Of India(AWBI) With recognition number - HR 337/ 2021. We provide our genuine services and also we have worked on many projects to control the dogs population.

Our Dream

It is our dream to control the diseases which are spread because of the stray dogs present all over the INDIA by strelization and vaccinating them against rabies. And also to help the every dog which is suffering from various diseases.

Our Goals

Our goal is to provide the best services from our side so that animals won't be hurted and they feel safe with us. We try our best to keep them happy and keep them away from diseases so that we human also can feel safe around them.
